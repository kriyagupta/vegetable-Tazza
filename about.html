About Tazza Veg

Tazza Veg is a plant-first café crafting seasonal, locally sourced dishes—delicious for you and kind to the planet.

Founded with a love for bold flavors and sustainable food, we blend traditional recipes with modern plant-based techniques to create nourishing meals that delight both vegans and non-vegans.

  • Our Mission: Make plant-based eating delicious, simple, and accessible for everyone.
  • What We Do: Daily café service, takeaway, catering, and seasonal pop-ups featuring local growers.
  • Our Values: 100% plant-based, locally sourced ingredients, seasonal menus, and low-waste practices.

From our kitchen to your table, every dish is crafted with care, seasonality, and a commitment to sustainability.

“We believe great food connects people—one thoughtful plate at a time.” — Founder, Tazza Veg

Visit us or order online—join our community of food lovers and change-makers.
